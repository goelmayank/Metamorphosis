About
Metamorphosis is a Short film-making competition, a platform for upcoming film-makers and artists to showcase their talents. It serves as a platform where every aspiring film-maker has the opportunity to amaze their viewers with their perspective, aesthetic sense, emotion or simple ingenuity of script writing.

Metamorphosis 2015 sees the evolution of the event into a Bigger, Better and a Tougher contest. With entries pouring in from every premiere college in India, and a bigger prize to be won, the stakes have never been higher.
SCRIPT
You need the simplest version of the idea-the one that will grow naturally in the subject's mind. Subtle art.
CAMERA
Pick up a camera. Shoot something. No matter how small, no matter how cheesy, no matter whether your friends and your family star in it.
EDIT
Its time to retrospect the movie and provide a finishing touch to your art.

RULES AND REGULATIONS
  • The maximum duration for an entry is 5 minutes.
  • The maximum number of members in a team is 5. (Excluding the cast)
  • Each entry should bear a) an appropriate title b) the team name.
  • A disclaimer bearing the following text : "This is an entry for Metamorphosis Oasis 2015.", the team name and title of the film must run for five seconds before the start of the video.
  • The video should contain the name of all the team members in the final credits along with contact number and email id of the team leader. The credits section and the disclaimer will not be counted in the 5 minutes allotted for the film.
  • All entries should be completely original and independently produced. Content and idea should be original. The participating teams themselves will be responsible for any third-party claim for copyright infringement.
  • Entries must reflect your direction and editing skills, and should comprise of cinematography and editing only.
  • Organizers solely reserve the right to screen the entry.
  • Pornography, nudity and Excessive Violence are strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Decision of the judges shall be final.
  • Keep checking the Facebook page for regular updates.
.
PARTICIPATION
1st Round :
  • This is a screening round. Selected best entries will be short listed for further rounds.
  • No specific theme is required.
  • The name of the movie file should follow the format : "metam14_$team name$_$title of film$".
  • Each entry must be sent along with an abstract not exceeding 100 words explaining the idea and the purpose of the film in .doc/.pdf file. The name of the .pdf/.doc file should also follow the above naming format.
  • The teams are required to mail their entries to metamorphosis[at]bits-oasis.org before the first round deadline. Teams from BITS Pilani may directly submit the entries to the undersigned.
  • After the qualified teams have submitted their video for the final round on the deadline day, they would be forwarded a questionnaire regarding their film which they must send back in 48 hours of receipt.
  • Videos shot using Digicams/Handycams/Mobile Phones are ALLOWED.
  • Last date for submitting first round entries of Metamorphosis is 23:59 17th Oct, 2015.
  • Please read the Rules and Regulations Section.
  • All teams must make sure that all team members are registered on the Oasis Registration Portal. Those who are NOT REGISTERED will not be registered.
2nd Round :
  • This will be a themed round.
  • The theme will be announced on 18th Oct, 2015.
  • Only those teams that are declared qualified will be eligible to participate in this round.
  • This final round will be an on-campus round. All teams are required to be present on campus , and submit their video entries to the undersigned. In special cases , the teams have the option of choosing a representative on campus to represent their team.
  • This round will include the screening of the videos for a panel of judges , followed by a question-answer session with individual teams. The teams may be judged on the basis of their performance in this session.
  • Videos shot using Digicams/Handy cams/Mobile Phones are ALLOWED.
  • Do look out for further notice regarding the deadline for the final round.
  • Please read the Rules and Regulations Section.
  • Last date for submitting second round entries of Metamorphosis is 23:59 28th Oct, 2015.
